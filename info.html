Information

How to use our site:

1. Search up the name of the Computer Science course you would like to select
2. Once you are on the courses page, to leave a review look to the right of the page to find the review section. To check already written reviews, scroll past the Description and underneath it you will find the already written reviews.

Topics to Cover in Your Review:

- Workload
- Need of textbooks
- Information on the Professor
- Presentation Type

The Purpose of this Site

This site is meant to help students at Loyola University Chicago in the Computer Science Program in leaving and reading reviews about courses being offered. By being able to understand how others felt in the class, we hope that the students will be able to make more educated choices when choosing courses. Through the act of leaving reviews, students can get a true understanding of a class if students in the current class feel that the description does not line up with what they do in class.
